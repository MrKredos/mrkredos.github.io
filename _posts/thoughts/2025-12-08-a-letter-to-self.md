>"Comparison is the thief of joy"

A quote that has stuck with me, and has reminded me that happiness is just as important as progressing your life. A quote that can be misconstrued as comparison being evil or not desirable, however is more commenting on excessive act of comparison and it's detriment to your mental. I'm not gonna deny that comparison is important for understanding where you are, and maybe finding direction in your life, but for someone like me that grew up non-stop comparing myself to people around me, younger and older, it has become a habit to unlearn rather than to take advantage of. 

My best advice is to not let others dictate YOUR life, and remind yourself that opinions are just unsolicited advice and you can treat them like that. Remind yourself that nobody is going to help you, and you don't need help either. Find yourself.
